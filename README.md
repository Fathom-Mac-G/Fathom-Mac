## 🔍 Overview

<a href="#fathom">Fathom</a> serves producers of ambient music, film and TV composers creating atmospheric texture, electronic music producers who work with evolving pad sounds, and sound designers who need complex, moving timbres that standard synthesizers don't produce.

The <a href="#fathom">ambient music production use case</a> uses Fathom for the slowly evolving pad sounds and atmospheric textures that define ambient music — sounds that shift continuously over long durations, developing timbral interest without rhythmic or melodic change, filling the frequency space that ambient music requires. The <a href="#fathom">film and TV scoring use case</a> applies Fathom to creating underscore atmospheres — tension textures, emotional backgrounds, environmental ambience, and the subtle sonic environments that support visual storytelling without drawing attention to themselves.

The <a href="#fathom">electronic music production use case</a> layers Fathom's evolving pads and spectral textures over rhythmic and melodic elements — providing the depth and atmosphere that electronic music production builds around rhythmic and harmonic foreground elements. The <a href="#fathom">sound design use case</a> explores Fathom's parameter space for distinctive sounds that no other synthesizer produces — the specific combinations of vector morphing, spectral processing, and granular texture that give Fathom its distinctive character.</p>

## 🤔 FAQ

<details>
<summary>Can I use hardware MIDI controllers with Fathom?</summary>

Yes. MIDI learn assigns synthesis parameters including vector position to hardware controller inputs for expressive performance control.

</details>

<details>
<summary>What music genres is Fathom suited for?</summary>

Ambient, cinematic, electronic, new age, experimental, and any genre where evolving textural pads and atmospheric sounds are relevant.

</details>

<details>
<summary>Does Fathom have granular synthesis?</summary>

Yes. Granular processing elements add granular texture and scatter effects over the vector synthesis core.

</details>

<details>
<summary>Can I layer multiple Fathom instances?</summary>

Yes. Multiple instances in the DAW build complex sounds from layered Fathom patches.

</details>

<details>
<summary>What is spectral processing in Fathom?</summary>

Spectral processing manipulates the frequency content of the synthesized signal — frequency shifting, spectral blurring, harmonic filtering — producing timbral transformations beyond what conventional filters achieve.

</details>
